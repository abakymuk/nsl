## Tone Guidelines for Recommendations

When writing copy recommendations:

**DO:**
- "We handle LA/LB daily" (confident, specific)
- "50 containers, 24/7 security" (facts, numbers)
- "Quote within 2 hours" (commitment, SLA)

**DON'T:**
- "We are passionate about logistics" (empty)
- "Your trusted partner" (claim without proof)
- "World-class service" (generic)
